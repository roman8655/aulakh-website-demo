Gender & Sexuality

Reaching out is a courageous step. With the right support, it is possible to feel more grounded, affirmed, and empowered in your identity.

Our clinicians' approach is grounded in evidence-based, gender-affirming, and LGBTQ+ affirmative care. This includes respectful and validating support of each person's gender identity; adapted Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT) to address minority stress, anxiety, depression, and internalized stigma; and trauma-informed care that recognizes the impact of discrimination and marginalization. We prioritize identity exploration without pressure toward any specific outcome, while fostering self-esteem, resilience, and a strong sense of self.

If you or someone you care about is navigating questions related to gender or sexuality, please know: you are not alone. You deserve care that respects and affirms who you are. Whether you are questioning, exploring, transitioning, or seeking support for related mental health concerns, we are here to walk alongside you.

Theraputic Process

Sessions 1-2

We begin by understanding your unique experience — your questions, concerns, goals, and the broader social and cultural context shaping your journey.

Sessions 3-4

From there, we collaboratively develop a plan that supports both identity exploration and overall mental health, strengthening coping skills, resilience, and self-compassion.

Sessions 5+

Subsequent sessions are scheduled at a frequency that aligns with your needs and preferences.

We also emphasize strengthening supportive relationships and community connections. For individuals experiencing gender dysphoria, care may include collaboration with medical providers and referrals aligned with established guidelines for gender-affirming treatment.

We recognize that gender and sexuality intersect with many aspects of life, including family, culture, relationships, and health care. Where appropriate, we can also provide documentation or referrals related to gender-affirming medical care and collaborate with other providers as part of a comprehensive support plan.

You deserve care that respects and affirms who you are. Whether you are questioning, exploring, transitioning, or seeking support for related mental health concerns, we are here to walk alongside you.
